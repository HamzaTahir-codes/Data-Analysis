What is data?

Data is the collection of facts, numbers, words, observations or other useful information.

What is Analysis?

Analysis is the detail examination of something complex in order to understand it's nature or to determine its essential features.

What is Data Analysis?

Data analysis converts raw data into actionable insights. 
It includes a range of tools, technologies, and processes used to find trends and solve problems using data.

e.g. If we have the numbers (1-100). At first it's in raw form. then we have a condition that we only want even numbers from this data. So we will divide the numbers by 2 to get the even numbers. Now this included a process to get the information.
And this process is called as Data Analysis.

What does the Data Analyst do?

A data analyst gathers information, look for meaningful patters, and share their findings with those they work with to help them make decisions.
